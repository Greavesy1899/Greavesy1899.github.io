Hello There!

My name is Connor Greaves and this is my portfolio which contains many projects of mine, with the exception of Skyblivion, which is a volunteer project. I am a third year student at the University of Lincoln, studying Games Computing with an expected grade of a first class honours.

Throughout university, I have gained a massive amount of knowledge; such as the ability to write fluently in C# and C++, develop procedural content in Unity, develop a game using an existing codebase such as Urho3d, use existing APIs and libraries such as PhysX, SDL and OpenGL, analyze algorithms and improve time and memory complexity, such as a boids algorithm.

When not studying, I have created projects such as the "Mafia II: Toolkit", which is a large collection of modding tools for Mafia II, this has developed my skills like reverse engineering, working with FbxSDK to build a pipeline for artists to import models into Mafia II, further knowledge of C#, and to take into account more of the users feedback, to improve usability and functionality.

Beneath you will find a collection of my projects ranging from 1st grade university projects, to fun side projects. The sidebar contains a categorised list of all my projects, based on their language/engine used. The "Uncategorised" section includes a variety of reverse engineering and mixed-language projects. The bottom of every page has my contact details.
