Hello There!

My name is Connor Greaves and this is my portfolio containing many projects I have developed or contributed too. Currently, I am a third year student at the University of Lincoln, studying Games Computing.

When I am not studying, my hobby and interests is mainly to develop my undestanding of reverse engineering and tool development to enable other people to modify these files. (see Mafia II: Toolkit for more). During certain projects, I have gained knowledge of some middleware software such as Scaleform, Kynapse and PhysX.

The sidebar contains a categorised list of all my projects, based on their language/engine used. The "Uncategorised" section includes a variety of reverse engineering and mixed-language projects.
